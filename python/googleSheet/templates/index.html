What is CaribCo?

Our mission is to provide a progressive living and work environment for creatives and digital nomads. Our shared office space provides your own dedicated desk-space and all the facilities you’d expect. But, beyond that, you’ll have the opportunity to interact with others to help you develop ideas and co-create. You become an independent part of a thriving, creative, energetic, co-working and living community. Our spaces are dedicated to freelancing designers, developers, web designers, programmers, video artists, game developers, and more…

Co-working

Follow your dreams. Join the global movement that's redefining the way we live, work and learn

Co-living

Whether you're flying solo, on a corporate retreat or with family in tow, we'll help you set the foundation for a life changing experience

Co-learning

Expand your career. Widen your horizon. Explore your sense of what's possible. For you and the world we live in. Come learn from industry luminaries in our international community.

Co-giving

We actively encourage our community of creative professionals to participate and give back to the local community in the form of training, workshops and mentoring. Contact us to learn more on how you can contribute to this growing community.
